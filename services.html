TUNING SERVICES

Professional calibration and performance solutions

ECU / FLASH TUNING

CORE SERVICE

Custom ECU calibration for factory and aftermarket engine management systems. We specialize in both read/write flash tuning for OEM ECUs and full standalone programming for race applications.

What We Tune

  • Factory ECU flash tuning (Hondata, COBB, EcuTek, HP Tuners, etc.)
  • Standalone ECU programming (Haltech, AEM, Link, MoTeC, Syvecs)
  • Piggyback tuning (AEM FIC, GrimmSpeed, etc.)
  • E85 / flex fuel conversion and tuning
  • Methanol injection integration
  • Anti-lag, launch control, flat-foot shifting

Tuning Process

All ECU tuning begins with a baseline pull and comprehensive data review. We calibrate fuel tables, ignition timing, boost control, and VVT across the entire operating range. Street tunes are refined through multiple road test iterations. Track cars receive additional development for race fuel, cooling systems, and competition-specific features.

Flash Tuning (OEM ECU) Starting at $600
Standalone ECU Setup & Tune Starting at $1,200

DYNO TUNING

PRECISION CALIBRATION

All performance tuning is validated on our Dynojet 424xLC2 AWD dynamometer with integrated wideband AFR monitoring and full exhaust extraction. We tune under load to optimize power delivery across the entire RPM range.

Dyno Services

  • Baseline power runs with full printout
  • Custom dyno tuning (naturally aspirated, turbo, supercharged)
  • Wideband AFR analysis and correction
  • Boost curve optimization and wastegate calibration
  • Drivetrain loss testing
  • Before/after modification testing

Our Equipment

Dynojet 424xLC2 with LinkECU capabilities, Innovate LM-2 dual wideband system, high-flow ventilation, and climate-controlled bay. All dyno sessions include comprehensive datalogging with EGT, boost pressure, and knock detection where applicable.

Baseline Power Run (3 pulls) $150
Dyno Tuning Session (per hour) $175/hr

PERFORMANCE DIAGNOSTICS

PROBLEM SOLVING

Advanced diagnostic services for modified vehicles. We troubleshoot tuning issues, verify sensor accuracy, identify mechanical problems, and resolve drivability concerns.

Diagnostic Capabilities

  • OBD-II and proprietary protocol scanning
  • Real-time datalog analysis
  • Wideband AFR verification and sensor testing
  • Boost leak testing and pressure verification
  • Compression and leak-down testing
  • Fuel pressure and flow testing
  • Ignition system diagnostics

When You Need Diagnostics

If your modified car is experiencing knock, running lean, misfiring, or showing unexpected power loss, diagnostics identify the root cause. We verify all sensor inputs, check fuel delivery, test ignition systems, and validate boost control before making tuning adjustments.
