ABOUT GT AUTOS

Experience, precision, and a proven track record

WHO WE ARE

GT Autos was founded in 2009 by lead calibrator Marcus Chen, a former Honda R&D engineer who spent five years developing factory turbo systems and ECU calibrations. After leaving the corporate world, Marcus set out to build a tuning shop focused on precision, data-driven results, and long-term reliability.

What started as a small operation tuning local Honda and Acura builds has grown into a full-service performance facility with expertise across Japanese, European, and domestic platforms. We've tuned everything from daily-driven street cars to dedicated time attack machines and SCCA competition builds.

Today, our facility houses a Dynojet 424xLC2 AWD dyno, complete fabrication capabilities, and a full complement of diagnostic and tuning equipment. We work with both OEM and standalone ECU systems, supporting everything from simple flash tunes to complete race car development.

OUR APPROACH

Performance tuning is about more than peak horsepower numbers. Our philosophy centers on three core principles: reliability, driveability, and measurable results.

Reliability First

Every tune prioritizes engine longevity. We use conservative safety margins, monitor knock activity closely, and validate AFR targets across all operating conditions. Street cars receive tunes designed for pump gas, varying fuel quality, and real-world driving conditions.

We don't guess. Every tuning decision is based on measured dataâ€”wideband AFR, knock sensors, EGT monitoring, and comprehensive datalogging. Our dyno provides real-time feedback that informs calibration adjustments.

Transparent Communication

Before any work begins, we discuss realistic expectations, potential limitations, and projected outcomes. We walk you through dyno results, explain tuning changes, and provide detailed documentation of all calibrations.
